Double Jeopardy

Identifying America’s most vulnerable communities through five years of air quality data

Using EPA AQI data from 2021–2025, we identified 37 counties where chronic pollution exposure and extreme air-quality emergencies collide — creating a compounding public-health risk.

Understanding Double Jeopardy

Chronic exposure reflects everyday air quality. Acute events capture the single worst days. Counties in the top 10% of both face Double Jeopardy.
