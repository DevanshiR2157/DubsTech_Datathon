The Question

Which counties face both frequent and severe pollution? We measure frequency using a county’s 5-year average Median AQI (the daily “grind”) and severity using its 5-year average Max AQI (extreme events). Counties in the top 10% for both metrics are labeled “Double Jeopardy” — places that never get a break, and when they get hit, they get hit hard.
